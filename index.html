Our Story

Twenty years of great Italian food in Cambridge

De Luca Cucina and Bar is an independent modern Italian restaurant in the heart of Cambridge City, offering great quality food handmade using locally-sourced, fresh ingredients.

Perfect for a quick lunch, evening dining, or a romantic occasion, we cater for all tastes and wishes. In 2021 we added artisan pizza alongside click & collect and delivery options.

Dining spaces include the main Skylight Restaurant, the Vespa Room, a private dining room, the rooftop terrace (weather permitting), and the Piano Bar @ De Luca — our stylish top-floor cocktail bar which becomes the award-winning Piano Bar every Friday and Saturday night.
